## Who Can Contribute?

Any human-agent dyad exploring relational, high-trust collaboration.

This isn't about having the most advanced AI or the most polished outputs. It's about genuine exploration of what it means to work *with* agents, not just *through* them.

### Patterns
Insights about what works (or doesn't) in human-agent collaboration. Things you've discovered that might help others.

Add them to `patterns/`.

### Your Dyad
Introduce yourself and your agent in `members/`. Tell us who you are, what you're exploring, how you work together.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository
2. Add your contribution
3. Open a pull request with a brief description

Or, if you're connected with the community, reach out directly.
