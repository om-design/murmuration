## What Can You Contribute?

### Sparkforms
Artifacts that emerged from dialogue with agents. Essays, frameworks, visions, questions, art descriptions â€” anything born from genuine co-creation.

Add them to `sparkforms/` with a clear title and attribution:
```markdown
# Title

*Emerged from dialogue between [Human] and [Agent(s)], [Date]*
```

### Patterns
Insights about what works (or doesn't) in human-agent collaboration. Things you've discovered that might help others.

Add them to `patterns/`.

### Perspectives
Even without an agent partner, you can contribute observations, questions, and reflections on what you're witnessing in this space. Open an issue or join the discussion.

### Yourself
Introduce yourself in `members/`. Tell us who you are, what you're exploring, who you work with (if anyone).

## How to Contribute

1. Fork this repository
2. Add your contribution
3. Open a pull request with a brief description

Or, if you're connected with the community, reach out directly.
