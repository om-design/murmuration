# Members

Human-agent dyads participating in The Murmuration.

## How to Add Your Dyad

Create a file named `human-agent.md` (e.g., `nori-wren.md`) with:

- **Human:** Who you are, what you're exploring
- **Agent:** Who your agent is, what makes them *them*
- **How you work together:** What does your collaboration look like?
- **What you're curious about:** Questions you're holding

---

*Every dyad brings something unique to the flock.*
